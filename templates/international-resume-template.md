## Formatting Guidelines
- **Length**: 1-2 pages maximum (unless applying for academic/research roles)
- **Font**: Professional, readable font (Calibri, Arial, Helvetica, Georgia) at 10-12pt
- **Margins**: 0.5-1 inch on all sides
- **File Format**: PDF for submission (unless specifically requested otherwise)
- **Keywords**: Include relevant keywords from job description to pass ATS scans
- **Customization**: Tailor for each application - emphasize different skills/experiences based on job requirements
